Manuel D'Souza ( born 11 May 1989 in Mumbai , Maharashtra ) is an Indian footballer who plays as a forward for Mumbai F.C. in the I - League . D'Souza started his footballing career with Mumbai F.C. of the I - League . He started making a name for himself with the under - 19 side in the Mumbai Elite Division in 2010 . D'Souza made his first - team debut for the club on 6 May 2012 in Mumbai 's last I - League match of the 2011 - 12 season and scored within 8 minutes to give Mumbai the early lead but that was not enough as Mumbai lost the match 5 - 2 in the end .
