His best individual result was 11th place on the pommel horse in 1936 . In 1929 Armelloni joined the military . Next year he was arrested for alleged communist propaganda activities among the military and in industrial plants . He was sentenced to two years in November 1931 and released on 28 October 1932 by an amnesty . As a result , he missed the 1932 Olympics while being a member of the national gymnastics team . Upon release he was asked to sign a letter denying his activities . He refused , and thus was also removed from the 1936 Olympic team . He was reinstated only by interference of Giorgio Vaccaro , Secretary - General of Italian Olympic Committee . After retiring from competitions Armelloni had a long career as gymnastics coach .
