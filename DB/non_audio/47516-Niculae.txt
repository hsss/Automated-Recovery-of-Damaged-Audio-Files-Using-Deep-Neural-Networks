Niculae Nedeff ( born September 26 , 1928 ) is a former Romanian handball player and a former coach of Romania women 's national handball team and Romania national handball team . He won the World Championships seven times , three times with the girls ' team and four times with the boys ' team . Born in Bucharest , Nedeff studied at Institutul de Cultura Fizica . After retiring , he joined the Executive Committee of the Romanian Olympic Committee .
